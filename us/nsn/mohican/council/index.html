  • Welcome to the new home of the Stockbridge-Munsee Tribal Code (previously referred to as the Tribal Ordinances). In 2021, the Tribal Ordinances were reorganized into the Stockbridge-Munsee Tribal Code. Ordinance chapters were renumbered and collected by subject into Titles with three-letter abbreviations.

  • To find the new location of an old ordinance, use the Ordinance Cross-References located as an attachment in the new Code.
