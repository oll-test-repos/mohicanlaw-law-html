Legislative History

1. Education ordinance adopted by Tribal Council per resolution no. 0689 dated February 19, 1980. Approved by BIA November 12, 1980.

2. Ordinance amended by resolution no. 1179 dated February 13, 1990 which created changes in Sections 1 and 10, changes incorporated.

3. Amended by Tribal Council on October 1, 1996, Resolution No. 078-96 inserting "grandparents" in Sections Section EDU.01.01, Section EDU.01.02, and Section EDU.01.03.

4. On October 2, 2012, the Tribal Council adopted Resolution No. 0004-13 amending Sections Section EDU.01.01, Section EDU.01.02, Section EDU.01.08 and Section EDU.01.09 of the ordinance.
