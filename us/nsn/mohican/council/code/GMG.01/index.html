Chapter GMG.01 — Gaming Ordinance

    A law to authorize, license and regulate certain forms of gaming, including Class II and Class III gaming, within the jurisdiction of the Stockbridge-Munsee Community on its Wisconsin Reservation.

Legislative History

1. Bingo Ordinance (No. 8) approved July 17, 1982.

2. Resolution No. 0817, adopted July 26, 1982.

3. Resolution No. 0928, adopted May 7, 1985, approved by BIA June 5, 1985.

4. Resolution No. 1009, adopted December 2, 1986.

5. Resolution No. 1064, adopted December 29, 1987.

6. Resolution No. 1081, adopted May 17, 1988.

7. Council action to change sections 5 and 6, November 1, 1988.

8. Resolution No. 1147, adopted May 16, 1989.

9. Gaming Ordinance entitled "An ordinance relating to the regulation of gaming activity on the Stockbridge-Munsee Reservation" passed by Council 1992.

10. Resolution No. 1338-92, adopted November 3, 1992.

11. May 1993, Gaming Roles and Responsibilities approved and added to ordinance.

12. Resolution No. 1370-93, adopted June 1, 1993.

13. Ordinance combined both Class II and Class III gaming, approved by Tribal Council August 19, 1993 by Resolution. Approved by National Indian Gaming Commission and published in Federal Register December 14, 1993.

14. March 21, 1995, Ordinance revised in its entirety, creating a Gaming Commission instead of a Gaming Board to regulate the gaming enterprise.

15. Resolution No. 1498-95, adopted May 24, 1995.

16. Council approved amendments to ordinance to reflect creation of Compliance Department and restructuring of Gaming Commission, June 28, 1995 - August 9, 1995. Those changes not affecting federally mandated provisions do not need NIGC approval and neither the Gaming Commission nor the Compliance Department are mandated by federal law.

17. July 14, 1995. Revised ordinance approved by NIGC.

18. Resolution No. 1525-95, adopted August 22, 1995.

19. Resolution No. 111-97, adopted February 18, 1997.

20. Resolution No. 232-97, adopted July 1, 1997.

21. Resolution No. 227-98, adopted June 2, 1998.

22. Resolution No. 026-99, adopted April 20, 1999.

23. Resolution No. 015-03, adopted April 1, 2003.

24. Resolution No. 043-03, adopted October 7, 2003, approved by NIGC December 11, 2003.

25. Resolution No. 057-09, adopted June 2, 2009. Amendments withdrawn prior to approval.

26. Resolution No. 060-09, adopted June 8, 2009, approved by NIGC June 12, 2009.

27. Resolution No. 012-12, adopted December 6, 2011, approved by NIGC March 5, 2012.

28. Resolution No. 02-16, adopted October 6, 2015. Amendments withdrawn prior to approval.

29. Resolution No. 018-16, adopted January 5, 2016. Amendments withdrawn prior to approval.

30. Resolution No. 029-16, adopted February 16, 2016, approved by NIGC May 6, 2016.
