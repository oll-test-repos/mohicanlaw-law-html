Stockbridge-Munsee Tribal Code

Section TAX.01.14 — Tax Receipts

(a) Tax Receipts And Disbursements. Except as otherwise provided herein, all monies received by the Tax Commission as Taxes shall be deposited immediately into the Tribal Tax Fund.

(b) Duties Of Tribal Treasurer. Except as otherwise provided herein, taxes collected under this Ordinance shall be paid into the Tribal Tax Fund.

(January 28, 2021, 026-18.)
