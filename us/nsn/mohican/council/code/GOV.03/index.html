Legislative History

1. Ordinance No. 2 Elections was approved May 16, 1939.

2. Section 2 was amended 1949 by deleting a provision to allow for a polling place in Red Springs.

3. Resolution No. 1526-95, adopted August 16, 1995.

4. Resolution No. 054-99, adopted August 17, 1999, approved by BIA November 1, 1999.

5. Resolution No. 00-056, adopted September 12, 2000, approved by BIA September 21, 2000.

6. Resolution No. 049-12, adopted August 21, 2012, approved by BIA September 5, 2012.

7. Ministerial corrections made in 2012 to correct citations and typos.

8. On October 14, 2017, the Tribe held an advisory referendum on whether to add a primary election to the annual election cycle. The majority of tribal members voting in the referendum supported adding the primary election.

9. Resolution No. 025-18, adopted March 6, 2018, approved by BIA on July 10, 2018.
