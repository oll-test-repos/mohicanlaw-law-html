Resolution No. 040-12 — Fish And Wildlife

Legislative History

The Tribal Council adopted the following changes to Chapter ENV.01:

  • Amended Section ENV.01.02(a), Section ENV.01.04, 21.15(B)(2), Section ENV.01.15.5(A), Section ENV.01.15.5(B), Section ENV.01.15.5(C), Section ENV.01.17(b), Section ENV.01.19(a), 21.20(V), Section ENV.01.21(a) and Section ENV.01.21(b); and
  • Deleted Section ENV.01.08(a)(1), Section ENV.01.08(c)(1), Section ENV.01.15(d), 21.15(E), Section ENV.01.15.5(D)-(G), 21.17(F)(2)(d), 21.17(G)(1) and 21.21(B)(3)(a)-(b); and
  • Created new Section ENV.01.02(b), Section ENV.01.12(c), Section ENV.01.12(d), 21.15(A)(1)(c), Section ENV.01.17(a)(1)-(3), 21.20(GG) and 21.21(B)(2)(a)-(b); and
  • Renumbered 21.17(F)(2)(e)-(g), 21.17(G)(2)-(4), Section ENV.01.19 and Section ENV.01.21; and
  • Technical change Section ENV.01.06(b), Section ENV.01.08(h), Section ENV.01.17(c) and 21.21(D)(1).
