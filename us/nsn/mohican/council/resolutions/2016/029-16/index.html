Resolution No. 029-16 — Gaming

Legislative History

Tribal Council adopted 2nd round of amendments, which incorporated the original 2015 revision and the January 2016 amendments. Tribal Council waived an additional posting period since changes are technical corrections required per NIGC. The adopted amendments are as follows:

  • Renumbering of entire ordinance.
  • Addition of Table of Contents.
  • Changes or additions to Sections: Section GMG.01.04(a), (I), (R), (S), (X), (Z) and (EE); Section GMG.01.08(n); Section GMG.01.09(h)(2)(B) and (e); Section GMG.01.09(h)(3)(A) and (c); Section GMG.01.09(k)(1); Section GMG.01.09(l); Section GMG.01.09(l)(1), (16), (20), (29), (30), (31)(d), (32)(a), (32)(a)(iv), (32)(c), 32(c)(i)-(iii) and (38); Section GMG.01.09(m)(1)(B); Section GMG.01.09(n); Section GMG.01.09(o); Section GMG.01.10(d)(1), (4), (4)(a)-(b); Section GMG.01.10(e)(1)-(3); Section GMG.01.10(f) and (F)(1), Section GMG.01.10(g), (H), (J) and (K); Section GMG.01.11(f), (F)(2) and (F)(5); Section GMG.01.14(p); Section GMG.01.15(b); Section GMG.01.15(c)(2)(O) and (9); Section GMG.01.15(d)(1) and (a)-(d); Section GMG.01.15(e); Section GMG.01.15(j)(6); Section GMG.01.15(k) and (1)-(3); Section GMG.01.15(l)(1)-(8); Section GMG.01.15(n); Section GMG.01.15(s); Section GMG.01.16(c); Section GMG.01.17(a), (A)(1)-(2), and (A)(7); and Section GMG.01.21(a).
  • Deletion of Section: 25.15(R)(8).
  • Addition of new Sections: Section GMG.01.04(t) and (U); Section GMG.01.07(g); Section GMG.01.15(d)(2); and Section GMG.01.15(j)(10).
  • Minor language clarifications in Sections: Section GMG.01.01(d); Section GMG.01.08(b), (G), (K) and (O); Section GMG.01.09(d); Section GMG.01.09(l)(3), (6), (11), (14), (19), (24), (25), (32)(1)(iii), and (33); Section GMG.01.10; Section GMG.01.12(a); Section GMG.01.14(a), (D), (E)(2)(b), (F)(2), (G) and (O); Section GMG.01.15(c)(4); Section GMG.01.15(d)(3)-(4); Section GMG.01.15(j); Section GMG.01.15(p)(6); Section GMG.01.17(a)(4); Section GMG.01.17(b); and Section GMG.01.21(c)(1).
Amendments submitted to NIGC for approval on February 17, 2016.
