Resolution No. 083-09 — Forest Resources

Legislative History

The proposed 2008 revisions were amended by the Tribal Council. The revisions include amending Sections 22.4 (Article I.B), 22.4 (Article II.A), Section ENV.02.10, Section ENV.02.12(a), Section ENV.02.12(b), Section ENV.02.12(c)(1), and 22.13 (C)(2); creating new Sections 22.12(A)(9), 22.12(A)(10), and 22.12(C)(8); deleting Section 22.12(B)(3); and renumbering Sections 22.12(B)(4-6). Approved by the BIA, except for Section ENV.02.24, and with the correction of a typo in the citation to 25 CFR in Section ENV.02.13. Section ENV.02.24, regarding the need for BIA approval of future amendments, was deleted.
