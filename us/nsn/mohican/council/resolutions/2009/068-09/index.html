Resolution No. 068-09 — Commercial-Security Interests

Legislative History

Amended Section BUS.05.01 and Section BUS.05.02; Created new Section BUS.05.01(a)(1), Section BUS.05.01(b), Section BUS.05.01(c), Section BUS.05.01(d), and Section BUS.05.01(e); and renumbered Section BUS.05.01 as Section BUS.05.01(a), Section GMG.02.04 as Section BUS.05.04, and Section GMG.02.05 as Section BUS.05.05.

WHEREAS, The Stockbridge-Munsee Community, Band of Mohican Indians, is a federally recognized Indian tribe with inherent sovereign powers, and is governed by the Stockbridge-Munsee Tribal Council pursuant to the 1937 Constitution;

WHEREAS, The Stockbridge-Munsee Community has the authority under its Constitution and its inherent rights as a sovereign nation to adopt and amend Ordinances;

WHEREAS, The Tribe adopted the New York Uniform Commercial Code ("UCC") as tribal law in relation to commercial security interests in Chapter 59 as a component of an agreement for the developing a casino in New York State;

WHEREAS, The Tribe now intends to obtain funding from a lender in relation to the completion of work on a hotel for the North Star Mohican Casino facility and the payment of outstanding amounts on previously completed work on the facility and such lender has requested that the Tribe amend Chapter 59 to address certain issues, including the applicability of the NY UCC in relation to activities in Wisconsin; and

WHEREAS, The Tribe wishes to amend Chapter 59 to address these issues.
