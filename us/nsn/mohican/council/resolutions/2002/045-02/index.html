Resolution No. 045-02 — Land

Legislative History

Several amendments and other modifications. Section PRP.01.01, Short Title is deleted, resulting in the other sections being renumbered. Other sections deleted were Section PRP.01.05 [now Section PRP.01.04] (B)(3), (B)(4), (B)(7), (B)(8), and Section PRP.01.06 [now Section PRP.01.05] (D), (G), (J). Sections amended were Section PRP.01.02 [now Section PRP.01.01], Section PRP.01.03 [now Section PRP.01.02] (A), (B), Section PRP.01.04 [now Section PRP.01.03] (A), (B), (C), (D), (E), Section PRP.01.05 [now Section PRP.01.04] (A)(1), (A)(3), (A)(4), (B)(1), (B)(2), (B)(5) [now (B)(3)], (B)(6) [now (B)(4)], (B)(9) [now (B)(5)], 40.6 (now 40.5) (A)(1), (A)(2), (B), (E) [now (D)], (H) [now (F)], (I) [now (G)].
